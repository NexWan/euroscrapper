Dark forces threaten Hyrule, and it's up to Link to save the day once again. Armed with powerful new abilities, Link will explore the vast open-world of Hyrule and the islands that float above it as he looks to fight back against the latest threat to the Kingdom. The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom is a direct sequel to Breath of the Wild and is exclusive to the Nintendo Switch.
